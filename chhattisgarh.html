Chhattisgarh

Brief History

Carved from Madhya Pradesh in 2000, Chhattisgarh is known for its dense forests, rich tribal culture, and ancient heritage with sites like Sirpur.

Major Places to Visit

  • Chitrakote & Tirathgarh Falls

    Why visit: Beautiful waterfalls in Bastar region.
    How to reach: Airport and Rail: Jagdalpur (~40 km); Road from Raipur (~300 km).

  • Kanger Valley National Park

    Why visit: Protected area with caves and waterfalls.
    How to reach: Airport/Rail: Jagdalpur; road access to park.

  • Sirpur (Buddhist/Temple Ruins)

    Why visit: Archaeological site with ancient temples.
    How to reach: Airport: Raipur (~80 km); Rail: Mahasamund (~30 km); road NH53.

Local Cuisine

Experience local dishes like Chila, Faraa, Bafauri, and Aamat. The tribal handicrafts including bell-metal works, dokra art, and kosa silk are cultural highlights.
