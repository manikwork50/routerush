Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Capital: Port Blair

Population: ~4.3 lakh (2023 est.)

Official Languages: Hindi, English, Tamil, Bengali, Telugu

History: This Union Territory consists of 572 islands, only a few of which are inhabited. Known for its marine diversity, coral reefs, tribal heritage, and pristine beaches, the islands were once home to the infamous British Cellular Jail. Today, it is a paradise for scuba diving, eco-tourism, and heritage lovers.
