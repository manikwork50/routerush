Himachal Pradesh

Brief History

Himachal Pradesh is famous for its stunning Himalayan landscapes, hill stations like Shimla and Manali, rich cultural heritage, and adventure tourism opportunities.

Major Places to Visit

  • Shimla

    Why visit: The summer capital of British India filled with colonial architecture and stunning natural beauty.
    How to reach: Airport: Jubbarhatti Shimla, rail: Kalka-Shimla toy train.

  • Manali

    Why visit: Adventure hub with skiing, trekking, and scenic valleys.
    How to reach: Airport: Kullu (Bhuntar), rail: Jogindernagar (far).

  • Dharamshala & McLeod Ganj

    Why visit: Tibetan culture and home of the Dalai Lama.
    How to reach: Airport: Gaggal, road from Pathankot.

  • Spiti Valley

    Why visit: High-altitude desert valley known for monasteries and rugged beauty.
    How to reach: Roads via Manali or Shimla during summer.

Local Cuisine

Sample traditional dishes like Dham Thali, Siddu, Chha Gosht, and Madra reflecting the region’s rich flavors.
