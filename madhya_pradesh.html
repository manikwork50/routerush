Madhya Pradesh

Capital: Bhopal

Population: ~85 million (2023 est.)

Official Language: Hindi

History: Known as the "Heart of India", Madhya Pradesh is home to a wealth of historical monuments, tiger reserves, tribal culture, and ancient temples. The state was formed in 1956 and holds cultural and geographical significance in India.
