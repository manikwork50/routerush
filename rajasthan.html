Rajasthan

Capital: Jaipur

Population: ~82 million (2023 est.)

Official Language: Hindi

History: Rajasthan, the "Land of Kings", is renowned for its royal palaces, majestic forts, colorful festivals, desert landscapes, and vibrant heritage. The state has deep Rajputana roots and attracts millions of tourists worldwide.

Jaipur

Location: Jaipur District

Famous for: Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, City Palace

How to reach: Jaipur Airport & Railway Station

Udaipur

Location: Udaipur District

Famous for: Lakes, palaces, and romance

How to reach: Udaipur Airport & Railway Station

Jaisalmer

Location: Jaisalmer District

Famous for: Golden Fort, desert safari

How to reach: Jaisalmer Railway Station, road from Jodhpur

Jodhpur

Location: Jodhpur District

Famous for: Mehrangarh Fort, Blue City

How to reach: Jodhpur Airport & Railway Station
