Andhra Pradesh

Capital: Amaravati (de facto), Visakhapatnam (executive)

Population: ~52 million (2023 est.)

Official Language: Telugu

History: Andhra Pradesh, formed in 1956, is known for its cultural richness, the Telugu film industry, historic temples, and long coastline along the Bay of Bengal. The Satavahana and Vijayanagara empires flourished here.
