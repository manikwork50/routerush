Bihar

Capital: Patna

Population: ~124 million (2023 est.)

Official Language: Hindi

History: Bihar is one of the oldest inhabited places in the world. It was the center of the ancient Magadha Empire, home to scholars like Chanakya and rulers like Ashoka. It's also a key site for Buddhism and Jainism, with Bodh Gaya being the place where Buddha attained enlightenment.
