Bihar

Brief History

Bihar is known as the ancient land of Magadha, famous for its Buddhist pilgrimage sites such as Bodh Gaya and the ruins of Nalanda University. It has a rich cultural heritage and historical significance.

Major Places to Visit

  • Bodh Gaya

    Why visit: Site of Buddha's enlightenment and Mahabodhi Temple.
    How to reach: Airport: Gaya (12 km); Rail: Gaya Junction (15 km); Road: Bus and taxi available from Patna (110 km).

  • Nalanda & Rajgir

    Why visit: Ancient Buddhist university ruins and historic sites.
    How to reach: Airport: Patna (90–100 km); Rail: Rajgir/Bihar Sharif; Road transport connects these towns.

  • Vaishali

    Why visit: Historic and archaeological site related to Lord Mahavira and Buddha.
    How to reach: Airport: Patna (60 km); Rail: Hajipur (35 km); Road connections available.

  • Patna (Golghar, museums)

    Why visit: Capital city with museums and historic sites like Golghar.
    How to reach: Airport and Rail available in city.

Local Cuisine

Popular dishes include Litti-chokha, sattu paratha, khaja sweets, thekua, and delicious malpua. Bihar is also known for its folk arts, including Madhubani paintings.
