Tamil Nadu

Brief History

Tamil Nadu is known for its rich temple architecture, ancient Dravidian culture, scenic hill stations, and flavorful South Indian cuisine.

Major Places to Visit

  • Meenakshi Temple, Madurai

    Why visit: Iconic multi-pillared temple and spiritual center.
    How to reach: Madurai International Airport and railway station accessible.

  • Marina Beach, Chennai

    Why visit: One of the longest urban beaches in the world.
    How to reach: Chennai International Airport and rail network.

  • Ooty

    Why visit: Popular hill station with botanical gardens and scenic viewpoints.
    How to reach: Nearest airport: Coimbatore; rail via Nilgiri Mountain Railway.

  • Mahabalipuram

    Why visit: UNESCO World Heritage site with ancient rock sculptures.
    How to reach: Chennai Airport, 60 km by road.

Local Cuisine

Tamil Nadu cuisine features dosa, idli, sambhar, filter coffee, and a variety of spicy vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes.
