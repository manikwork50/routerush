Uttarakhand

Capital: Dehradun (Winter), Gairsain (Summer)

Population: ~11 million (2023 est.)

Official Language: Hindi

History: Uttarakhand, known as "Devbhumi" (Land of the Gods), is home to the majestic Himalayas, ancient temples, and sacred rivers. It was carved out of Uttar Pradesh in 2000 and is a major center for yoga, pilgrimage, and trekking.
