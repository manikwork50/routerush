Meghalaya

Capital: Shillong

Population: ~3.8 million (2023 est.)

Official Languages: English (official), Khasi, Garo

History: Meghalaya, meaning "abode of clouds," became a full-fledged state in 1972. Known for heavy rainfall, rolling hills, waterfalls, and tribal traditions, it is home to the Khasi, Garo, and Jaintia tribes who preserve their matrilineal culture.

Shillong

Location: Central Meghalaya

Famous for: Lakes, hills, cafes, Scotland of the East

How to reach: Closest airport: Umroi (30 km), road from Guwahati

Famous for: One of the wettest places on Earth, caves, waterfalls

How to reach: Shared taxis or private cab from Shillong
