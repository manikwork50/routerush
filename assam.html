Location: Golaghat & Nagaon districts

Famous for: One-horned rhinoceroses, tigers, elephants

How to reach: Nearest airport: Jorhat (97 km); by road from Guwahati

Majuli Island

Location: Brahmaputra River

Famous for: World's largest river island, satras, tribal culture

How to reach: Ferry from Neemati Ghat near Jorhat

Kamakhya Temple

Location: Nilachal Hill, Guwahati

Famous for: Powerful Shakti Peeth, Ambubachi Mela

How to reach: 6 km from Guwahati Railway Station

Sivasagar

Location: Eastern Assam

Famous for: Ahom monuments, Sivadol Temple, tanks

How to reach: Nearest airport: Jorhat (75 km); rail from Guwahati

Manas National Park

Location: Western Assam (Barpeta)

Famous for: UNESCO site, tigers, elephants, golden langur

How to reach: Nearest rail: Barpeta Road (22 km); Guwahati by road

Haflong

Location: Dima Hasao district

Tezpur

Location: Sonitpur District

Dipor Bil

Location: Guwahati outskirts
