Manipur

Brief History

Manipur is a picturesque land known for Loktak Lake, vibrant tribal cultures, rich historical sites, and lively markets like the unique all-women’s market, Ima Keithel.

Major Places to Visit

  • Loktak Lake

    Why visit: The largest freshwater lake in Northeast India with floating phumdis.
    How to reach: Imphal Airport; well connected by road.

  • Kangla Fort

    Why visit: Historic fort and cultural center in Imphal.
    How to reach: Located in Imphal city.

  • Manipur State Museum

    Why visit: Displays tribal arts, history, and culture.
    How to reach: In Imphal city.

  • Ima Keithel Market

    Why visit: Unique all-women’s market selling local produce and crafts.
    How to reach: Imphal city.

Local Cuisine

Enjoy traditional dishes such as Eromba, Chak-hao rice, and fish preparations reflecting the rich Manipuri heritage.
