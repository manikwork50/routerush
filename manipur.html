Manipur

Capital: Imphal

Population: ~3.1 million (2023 est.)

Official Languages: Meitei (Manipuri), English

History: Manipur, known as the "Jewel of India", joined the Indian Union in 1949 and became a state in 1972. Rich in tribal and classical culture, dance, and natural beauty, it is the birthplace of Manipuri dance and the game of modern polo.

Loktak Lake

Location: Moirang, 48 km from Imphal

Famous for: Floating phumdis, Keibul Lamjao National Park

How to reach: By road from Imphal
