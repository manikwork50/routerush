Arunachal Pradesh

Capital: Itanagar

Population: ~1.6 million (2023 est.)

Official Language: English

History: Arunachal Pradesh, the "Land of the Rising Sun," became a full-fledged state in 1987. Known for its tribal culture, natural beauty, and unexplored Himalayan terrain, it shares international borders with Bhutan, China, and Myanmar.
