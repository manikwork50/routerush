Goa

Capital: Panaji

Population: ~1.6 million (2023 est.)

Official Languages: Konkani (official), Marathi, English

History: Goa, a former Portuguese colony, became part of India in 1961. Known for its beaches, nightlife, Portuguese architecture, churches, and seafood, Goa is one of the most visited tourist destinations in India, blending heritage and coastal charm.
