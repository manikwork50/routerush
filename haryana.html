Haryana

Capital: Chandigarh

Population: ~29 million (2023 est.)

Official Language: Hindi

History: Haryana is the land of the epic Mahabharata battlefield, Kurukshetra. With a rich Vedic history, it is also a major agricultural and industrial hub, bordering Delhi and offering a blend of heritage, spirituality, and modernity.

Kurukshetra

Location: Kurukshetra District

Famous for: Mahabharata battlefield, Brahma Sarovar

How to reach: Kurukshetra Railway Station; road from Delhi (160 km)

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary

Location: Gurgaon District

Famous for: Migratory birds, nature walks

How to reach: 50 km from Delhi by road

Pinjore Gardens

Location: Panchkula District

Famous for: Mughal-style garden complex

How to reach: Near Chandigarh (22 km)

Morni Hills

Location: Panchkula

Famous for: Scenic hill station, boating, trekking

How to reach: 45 km from Chandigarh

Karnal

Location: Central Haryana

Famous for: Karnal Lake, historic Mughal bridge

How to reach: Karnal Railway Station; NH 44 from Delhi

Panipat

Location: Northern Haryana

Famous for: Historic battlefields, Panipat Museum

How to reach: Panipat Junction by train, NH 44

Bhindawas Sanctuary

Location: Jhajjar District

Famous for: Wetland birds, peaceful environment

How to reach: 100 km from Delhi by road

Agroha Dham

Location: Hisar District

Famous for: Hindu temple complex, cultural site

How to reach: 20 km from Hisar city; bus/taxi

Surajkund

Location: Faridabad

Famous for: International crafts mela, folk art

How to reach: 20 km from Delhi; easily accessible by road

Rock Garden

Location: Near Chandigarh

Famous for: Sculptures made of waste materials

How to reach: Chandigarh city; accessible by rail, road, air
