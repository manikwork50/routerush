Haryana

Brief History

Haryana offers a blend of ancient heritage and urban growth, known for sites linked with the Mahabharata, architectural marvels, and rich cultural traditions.

Major Places to Visit

  • Kurukshetra

    Why visit: Known as the ‘Land of the Bhagavad Gita,’ with spiritual and historical sites.
    How to reach: Road and rail accessible from Delhi (~170 km).

  • Sultanpur National Park

    Why visit: Bird sanctuary with migratory birds.
    How to reach: Near Gurugram; accessible from Delhi.

  • Pinjore Gardens

    Why visit: Well-maintained Mughal-style gardens.
    How to reach: Near Chandigarh, road accessible.

  • Morni Hills

    Why visit: Pleasant hill station with rich natural beauty.
    How to reach: About 45 km from Chandigarh by road.

Local Cuisine

Famous for Bajra Khichdi, Kadhi Pakora, Butter Chicken, and other traditional North Indian dishes with rich flavors.
