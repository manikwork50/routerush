Jammu & Kashmir

Brief History

Jammu & Kashmir is famed for its stunning landscapes including the Dal Lake, majestic Himalayas, Mughal gardens, and vibrant Kashmiri culture and craft traditions.

Major Places to Visit

  • Dal Lake, Srinagar

    Why visit: Picturesque lake famous for houseboats and shikara rides.
    How to reach: Srinagar Airport (~12 km); well connected by road.

  • Gulmarg

    Why visit: Skiing and adventure destination with scenic gondola rides.
    How to reach: 50 km from Srinagar by road.

  • Pahalgam

    Why visit: Popular hill station with beautiful valleys and trekking trails.
    How to reach: 90 km from Srinagar by road.

  • Sonamarg

    Why visit: Known for glaciers and scenic beauty.
    How to reach: 80 km from Srinagar by road (seasonal).

Local Cuisine

Taste the famous Wazwan dishes including Rogan Josh and Gushtaba, along with traditional Kashmiri breads and saffron-infused dishes.
