Himachal Pradesh

Capital: Shimla (Summer), Dharamshala (Winter)

Population: ~7.4 million (2023 est.)

Official Language: Hindi

History: Nestled in the western Himalayas, Himachal Pradesh is known for its scenic beauty, colonial-era towns, spiritual destinations, and adventure sports. It became a full-fledged state of India in 1971.
