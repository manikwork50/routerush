Telangana

Brief History

Telangana, carved from Andhra Pradesh in 2014, is known for Hyderabad city, rich heritage of the Golconda Sultanate, and its unique Telangana culture and cuisine.

Major Places to Visit

  • Charminar, Hyderabad

    Why visit: Iconic 16th-century monument and mosque.
    How to reach: Rajiv Gandhi International Airport; well-connected by metro and road.

  • Golconda Fort

    Why visit: Majestic ruins of medieval fort and mining city.
    How to reach: 11 km from Hyderabad city center by road.

  • Ramoji Film City

    Why visit: World’s largest integrated film studio and theme park.
    How to reach: About 30 km from Hyderabad, accessible by road.

  • Hussain Sagar Lake

    Why visit: Large lake with Buddha statue and recreational activities.
    How to reach: Located in Hyderabad city center.

Local Cuisine

Telangana cuisine features Hyderabadi Biryani, Mirchi ka Salan, Pesarattu, and other spicy and flavorful dishes reflecting Nizami traditions.
