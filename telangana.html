Telangana

Capital: Hyderabad

Population: ~38 million (2023 est.)

Official Language: Telugu

History: Telangana, India's youngest state formed in 2014, has a rich legacy of the Kakatiya, Qutb Shahi, and Nizam dynasties. The region is known for its unique blend of South and Deccan cultures, rich history, architecture, and spicy cuisine.
