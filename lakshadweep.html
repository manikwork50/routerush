Lakshadweep

Brief History

Lakshadweep is a group of coral islands known for their pristine beaches, coral reefs, vibrant maritime culture, and calm island life.

Major Places to Visit

  • Kavaratti Island

    Why visit: Administrative capital with beautiful lagoons and a mosque.
    How to reach: By boat or helicopter from Kochi.

  • Bangaram Island

    Why visit: Coral islands with great snorkeling and diving opportunities.
    How to reach: Accessible by boat from Kavaratti.

  • Minicoy Island

    Why visit: Known for its lighthouse and distinct culture.
    How to reach: Flights from Kochi to Minicoy or by boat.

Local Cuisine

Lakshadweep cuisine is rich in seafood, coconut, and rice-based dishes with influences from Kerala and Maldives.
