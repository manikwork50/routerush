Dadra and Nagar Haveli

Brief History

Dadra and Nagar Haveli is a union territory known for its lush green nature, tribal culture, waterfalls, lakes, and colonial heritage including churches and temples.

Major Places to Visit

  • Vanganga Lake Garden

    Why visit: Beautiful landscaped gardens adjacent to a serene lake.
    How to reach: Located in Silvassa, the capital of the union territory.

  • Hirwa Van Garden

    Why visit: Known for waterfalls, flower fields and peaceful nature trails.
    How to reach: Piparia, near Silvassa.

  • Dudhni Lake

    Why visit: Popular for boating and picnicking.
    How to reach: Near Silvassa, accessible by road.

  • Vasona Lion Safari

    Why visit: Mini safari featuring Asiatic lions.
    How to reach: Silvassa Wildlife Sanctuary.

  • Satmaliya Deer Park

    Why visit: Deer varieties and natural surroundings.
    How to reach: Near Silvassa.

  • Tribal Museum, Silvassa

    Why visit: Authentic displays of tribal culture, crafts, and lifestyle.
    How to reach: Silvassa city center.

Local Cuisine

The cuisine reflects tribal and Gujarati influences - fresh river fish, coconut-based dishes, and local sweets are popular here.
