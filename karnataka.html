Location: Bangalore Urban

Mysore

Location: Mysuru District

Famous for: Mysore Palace, Dussehra Festival

How to reach: Mysuru Junction, road from Bengaluru (150 km)

Hampi

Location: Vijayanagara District

Famous for: Ruins of Vijayanagara Empire

Coorg

Location: Kodagu District

Famous for: Coffee estates, hills, waterfalls

How to reach: Road from Mysore (115 km); no rail/air

Gokarna

Location: Uttara Kannada

Famous for: Pilgrimage & secluded beaches

How to reach: Ankola Railway (20 km); road from Goa or Karwar

Badami

Location: Bagalkot District

Famous for: Cave temples & Chalukya architecture

Chikmagalur

Location: Western Ghats

Famous for: Coffee plantations, Mullayanagiri peak

How to reach: Road from Bengaluru (245 km)

Jog Falls

Location: Shivamogga District

Famous for: Hoysala temples, intricate stone carvings
