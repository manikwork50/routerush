Karnataka

Brief History

Karnataka is known for its rich history with the Vijayanagara Empire, the royal city of Mysore, Hampi ruins, and lively cosmopolitan Bangalore city.

Major Places to Visit

  • Mysore Palace

    Why visit: Magnificent royal palace and cultural heritage.
    How to reach: Mysore Airport; Rail and road connectivity from Bangalore (150 km).

  • Hampi

    Why visit: UNESCO World Heritage site with ancient ruins.
    How to reach: Airport: Hubli (nearby); Rail: Hospet.

  • Coorg

    Why visit: Scenic hill station with coffee plantations and waterfalls.
    How to reach: Mangalore Airport (90 km); road from Mysore.

  • Bangalore (Bengaluru)

    Why visit: IT hub with botanical gardens and vibrant nightlife.
    How to reach: Kempegowda International Airport; excellent road and rail network.

Local Cuisine

Enjoy Bisi Bele Bath, Mysore Pak, Ragi Mudde and a variety of dosas reflecting Karnataka’s culinary diversity.
