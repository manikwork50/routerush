Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Brief History

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands have a rich history, including being a colonial exile site known as Kala Pani with the Cellular Jail memorial. The islands have a unique tribal culture and are renowned for their pristine beaches and natural beauty.

Major Places to Visit

  • Cellular Jail

    Why visit: A historic colonial prison and national memorial.
    How to reach: Port Blair Airport; accessible within Port Blair city.

  • Radhanagar Beach (Havelock Island)

    Why visit: Awarded Asia’s Best Beach, famous for white sands and crystal-clear waters.
    How to reach: Ferry from Port Blair (2-3.5 hours).

  • Ross Island

    Why visit: Historic British colonial ruins and mascot deer population.
    How to reach: Short boat ride from Port Blair.

  • North Bay Island

    Why visit: Water sports, coral reefs, and marine life.
    How to reach: Ferry from Port Blair.

Local Cuisine

The cuisine features fresh seafood, coconut-based dishes, and tribal specialties. Try grilled fish, crab preparations, and local tropical fruits.
