West Bengal

Brief History

West Bengal has a rich cultural heritage, shaped by colonial architecture, Bengali Renaissance, and vibrant traditional arts and festivals.

Major Places to Visit

  • Kolkata

    Cultural capital known for the Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, Indian Museum, and festivals like Durga Puja.
    How to reach: Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport; Howrah and Sealdah railway stations.

  • Darjeeling

    Famous hill station with panoramic views of Kanchenjunga, tea gardens, and the UNESCO Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.
    How to reach: Bagdogra Airport and New Jalpaiguri railway station, then road to Darjeeling.

  • Sundarbans

    Largest mangrove forest home to the Royal Bengal Tiger and unique ecosystem.
    How to reach: Nearest airport: Kolkata; gateways at Gosaba and Canning by road and river.

  • Shantiniketan

    The university town founded by Rabindranath Tagore, known for art, culture, and dance traditions.
    How to reach: Bolpur Santiniketan railway station; road from Shantiniketan.

Local Cuisine

Bengali cuisine is famous for fish curry, sweets like Rosogolla and Sandesh, and traditional thalis with rice and lentils.
