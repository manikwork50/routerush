Puducherry

Brief History

Puducherry was a former French colonial territory known for its serene French Quarter, spiritual hub at Auroville, and scenic beaches. It holds a unique blend of Franco-Tamil culture.

Major Places to Visit

  • White Town Promenade

    Why visit: French Colonial architecture, tasty cafes and a scenic walkway.
    How to reach: Nearest airport: Puducherry (PNY), 10 km; from Chennai Airport ~150 km by road; Rail: Villupuram, 35 km.

  • Auroville

    Why visit: International spiritual community with unique architecture and communal living.
    How to reach: Road from Puducherry town, about 12 km.

  • Paradise and Serenity Beaches

    Why visit: Relaxing clean beaches less crowded than other coastal areas.
    How to reach: Local buses and taxis easily accessible from Puducherry.

  • Arikamedu

    Why visit: Ancient Roman trade site with archaeological ruins.
    How to reach: By road approx 7 km from Puducherry town.

Local Cuisine

Puducherry cuisine combines Tamil and French influences — try Meen Kuzhambu (spicy fish curry), Creole seafood, and delicious French-style bakeries and pastries.
