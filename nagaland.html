Nagaland

Capital: Kohima

Population: ~2.2 million (2023 est.)

Official Languages: English (widely used), Nagamese, various tribal languages

History: Nagaland became the 16th state of India in 1963. Known for its vibrant tribal culture, traditional festivals like Hornbill, and lush hills, Nagaland is one of the most culturally rich states in India. Over 16 major tribes inhabit this state.
