Tamil Nadu

Capital: Chennai

Population: ~78 million (2023 est.)

Official Language: Tamil

History: Tamil Nadu is one of the oldest surviving civilizations with deep roots in Dravidian culture, Sangam literature, and temple architecture. It's renowned for its classical music, dance (Bharatanatyam), temples, and beaches.

Meenakshi Temple

Location: Madurai

Famous for: Stunning Dravidian architecture, temple towers

How to reach: Madurai Airport or Railway Station

Marina Beach

Location: Chennai

Famous for: Longest natural urban beach in India

How to reach: Chennai Central or Airport

Mahabalipuram

Location: 60 km south of Chennai

Famous for: Shore Temple, rock-cut architecture, UNESCO site

How to reach: Bus or taxi from Chennai

Kanyakumari

Location: Southernmost tip of India

Famous for: Triveni Sangam, sunrise/sunset views

How to reach: Kanyakumari Railway Station

Ooty

Location: Nilgiris

Famous for: Hill station, toy train, botanical gardens

How to reach: Mettupalayam + toy train or road

Rameswaram

Location: Pamban Island

Famous for: Jyotirlinga temple, pilgrimage

How to reach: Rameswaram Railway Station

Chettinad

Location: Sivaganga District

Famous for: Heritage mansions, spicy cuisine

How to reach: Karaikudi Railway Station

Thanjavur

Location: Thanjavur District

Famous for: Brihadeeswarar Temple (UNESCO), paintings

How to reach: Thanjavur Railway Station

Yelagiri Hills

Location: Vellore District

Famous for: Quiet hill station, nature treks

How to reach: Jolarpettai Junction (21 km)

Vellore Fort

Location: Vellore

Famous for: Granite fort, Jalakandeswarar Temple

How to reach: Vellore Railway Station
