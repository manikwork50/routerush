Chandigarh

Capital: Chandigarh (also serves as capital of Punjab & Haryana)

Population: ~1.2 million (2023 est.)

Official Languages: Hindi, Punjabi, English

History: Chandigarh was designed by Swiss-French architect Le Corbusier and established as a planned city post-independence to serve as Punjab's capital. It's known for its architecture, green cover, and urban planning.
