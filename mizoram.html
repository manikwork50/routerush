Mizoram

Brief History

Mizoram is renowned for its rolling hills, lush green landscapes, vibrant tribal festivals, and unique cultural traditions.

Major Places to Visit

  • Dzukou Valley

    Why visit: Beautiful valley known for trekking and seasonal flowers.
    How to reach: Nearest airport: Dimapur; road access via Kohima.

  • Aizawl

    Why visit: Capital city with scenic viewpoints and cultural museums.
    How to reach: Lengpui Airport, well connected by road.

  • Palak Lake

    Why visit: Famous freshwater lake surrounded by hills.
    How to reach: Accessible via road from Aizawl.

  • Reiek Hills

    Why visit: Hill station with panoramic views and tribal villages.
    How to reach: Near Aizawl city, accessible by road.

Local Cuisine

Try traditional Mizoram dishes like Bai (vegetable stew), Vawksa Rep (smoked pork), and local rice beer reflecting the state’s culinary heritage.
