Mizoram

Capital: Aizawl

Population: ~1.2 million (2023 est.)

Official Languages: Mizo, English, Hindi

History: Mizoram, meaning "land of the Mizos," became a state in 1987. It is known for its vibrant tribal culture, lush green hills, and peaceful cities. The majority of the population belongs to various Mizo tribes, who follow rich cultural traditions, dances, and festivals like Chapchar Kut.

How to reach: Lengpui Airport (32 km), taxis and buses available
