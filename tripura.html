Tripura

Brief History

Tripura is a land of palaces, dense hills, diverse tribal culture, and rich heritage with beautiful wildlife sanctuaries and ancient rock carvings.

Major Places to Visit

  • Ujjayanta Palace

    Why visit: Former royal palace now a state museum showcasing Tripura’s history.
    How to reach: Agartala Airport and railway station nearby.

  • Neermahal Palace

    Why visit: Water palace located in Rudrasagar Lake, blending Hindu and Mughal architecture.
    How to reach: Accessible from Agartala by road.

  • Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary

    Why visit: Home to a variety of tropical flora and fauna including primates.
    How to reach: 25 km from Agartala, well connected by road.

  • Unakoti

    Why visit: Historic rock-cut sculptures and pilgrimage site.
    How to reach: Road access from Kailashahar.

  • Jampui Hills

    Why visit: Scenic hills known for orange orchards and tribal villages.
    How to reach: Accessible by road from North Tripura district.

Local Cuisine

Try Tripuri dishes like Bamboo shoot pork, Chakhwi, and a variety of preparations using fresh forest produce and local herbs.
