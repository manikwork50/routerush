Gujarat

Brief History

Gujarat is a land of rich heritage, famous for the Statue of Unity honoring Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, ancient architectural wonders like Rani ki Vav, and vibrant cultural festivals.

Major Places to Visit

  • Statue of Unity

    Why visit: World's tallest statue honoring Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.
    How to reach: Closest airport: Vadodara, 90 km; Rail: Kevadia Station.

  • Gir National Park

    Why visit: Last refuge of Asiatic lions and rich wildlife.
    How to reach: Airports in Rajkot, Diu; Rail: Junagadh.

  • Somnath Temple

    Why visit: One of the 12 Jyotirlinga shrines.
    How to reach: Airport: Diu; Rail: Veraval.

  • Rann of Kutch

    Why visit: Largest salt desert famous for Rann Utsav festival.
    How to reach: Airport: Bhuj; Rail: Bhuj.

  • Dwarkadhish Temple

    Why visit: Sacred Hindu pilgrimage site on Arabian Sea.
    How to reach: Airport: Jamnagar; Rail: Dwarka station.

Local Cuisine

Gujarat offers flavorsome vegetarian dishes like Undhiyu, Khaman, Thepla, along with exquisite sweets and vibrant handicrafts such as bandhani and patola silk.
