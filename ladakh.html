Ladakh

Brief History

Ladakh is renowned for its dramatic Himalayan landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and unique Ladakhi culture influenced by Tibetan heritage.

Major Places to Visit

  • Hemis Monastery

    Why visit: Largest and richest Buddhist monastery in Ladakh.
    How to reach: Leh Airport, then 45 km by road.

  • Pangong Lake

    Why visit: Stunning high-altitude lake famous for its changing colors.
    How to reach: Around 160 km from Leh by road.

  • Thiksey Monastery

    Why visit: Beautiful twelve-story monastery with impressive architecture.
    How to reach: 19 km from Leh, accessible by road.

  • Nubra Valley

    Why visit: Scenic valley known for sand dunes, Bactrian camels, and monasteries.
    How to reach: Approximately 150 km from Leh via Khardung La Pass.

Local Cuisine

Ladakhi cuisine includes dishes like Thukpa, Momos, Skyu (noodle soup), and Chhang (traditional drink) reflecting Tibetan influences.
