Ladakh

Capital: Leh

Population: ~300,000 (2023 est.)

Official Languages: Ladakhi, Hindi, English

History: Ladakh became a Union Territory in 2019, carved out from Jammu & Kashmir. Known as the "Land of High Passes", it offers dramatic landscapes, monasteries, and Himalayan adventures. Leh and Kargil are the key districts. The region is culturally linked to Tibetan Buddhism and is a top destination for bikers and trekkers.
