Madhya Pradesh

Brief History

Madhya Pradesh is known as the 'Heart of India', rich in heritage sites, home to ancient temples, the Khajuraho group of monuments, and abundant wildlife sanctuaries.

Major Places to Visit

  • Khajuraho Temples

    Why visit: Famous UNESCO World Heritage erotic sculptures.
    How to reach: Airports at Khajuraho; direct trains available.

  • Bandhavgarh National Park

    Why visit: Tiger reserve with rich biodiversity.
    How to reach: Airports: Jabalpur, Katni; Rail: Umaria.

  • Sanchi Stupa

    Why visit: Ancient Buddhist complex and UNESCO site.
    How to reach: Rail/road from Bhopal (~46 km).

  • Bhimbetka Caves

    Why visit: Prehistoric rock art and caves.
    How to reach: Near Bhopal, accessible by road.

  • Pachmarhi

    Why visit: Hill station with waterfalls and pine forests.
    How to reach: Nearest airport: Bhopal; rail: Pipariya.

Local Cuisine

Enjoy traditional dishes like Poha, Bhutte ka Kees, Dal Bafla, and sweets like Jalebi reflecting the region’s rich culinary traditions.
